How to Pre-order the Nintendo Switch 2

To secure your preorder for the Nintendo Switch 2, here's a breakdown of the typical process and current availability:

Official Nintendo Channels:

  • My Nintendo Store (via Invitation): Nintendo implemented an invitation system for pre-orders through their official My Nintendo Store. Invitations were primarily sent out to Nintendo Account holders who had an active 12-month Nintendo Switch Online membership and at least 50 hours of gameplay logged before April 2, 2025.
  • Nintendo Store Physical Locations: While the Nintendo Stores in New York and San Francisco do not typically take in-store pre-orders, check their websites for launch day availability.

Major Retailers (Online and In-Store):

  • Walmart: Pre-orders were available online, with some promising launch day delivery for early orders placed before 8:00 AM ET on June 4th. Check for restocks.
  • Target: Online pre-orders were available but are currently sold out. Monitor their website for availability.
  • Best Buy: Announced midnight launch events for pre-order pickups.
  • GameStop: Online and in-store pre-orders available. Additional units expected June 4th, with sales starting at 3 PM local time.
  • Amazon: Invite-only pre-order system. Check your inbox for eligibility.
  • UK Retailers: Very, Currys, ShopTo, and others had pre-order bundles, though availability fluctuates.

Tips for Securing a Preorder (Ongoing):

  • Check retailer websites frequently.
  • Sign up for restock alerts or app notifications.
  • Follow trusted gaming news sources and retailers on social media for real-time updates.
  • Act quickly—stock tends to sell out within minutes.

Important Considerations:

  • Retailers may offer bundles that include games or accessories. These may have better availability.
  • Some stores will offer limited quantities on launch day (June 5, 2025). Contact stores to confirm their procedures.

Given the high demand, persistence and timing are key to securing a Nintendo Switch 2 preorder.
