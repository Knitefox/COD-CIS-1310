Technical Specifications

Processor: Custom NVIDIA Tegra X2 Chipset

Display: 7.5-inch LCD, 1080p resolution (handheld), 4K output (docked)

RAM: 12GB LPDDR5

Storage: 256GB internal, expandable via microSDXC

Battery Life: Up to 8 hours (varies by game)

Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, USB-C

Other Features: Backward compatibility with all Nintendo Switch games, enhanced Joy-Con with improved drift resistance
